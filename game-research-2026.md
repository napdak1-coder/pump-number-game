## Section 6: Game "Juice" and Feel - Making PUMP NUMBER Irresistibly Satisfying

### What is Game Juice?

"Juice" or "game feel" is the **tactile virtual sensation** of interacting with the game. It decides how fun the second-to-second play feels. Juicing doesn't change the rules芒聙聰it changes how the game *feels*.

**Modern games make players feel the impact of their actions through:**
- Dynamic audio-visual feedback
- Responsive animations
- Haptic reinforcement
- Immediate, varied responses

### Three Pillars of Juice Implementation

**1. Sound Design (Most Underutilized)**

Audio is the fastest way to improve perceived game quality. Good sounds make actions feel credible and present.

*For PUMP NUMBER - Sound Design Strategy:*

**Match Sounds:**
- Different pitch for each number (1-9)
  - 1 = 200Hz (low)
  - 5 = 400Hz (mid)
  - 9 = 600Hz (high)
- Duration: 80-150ms
- Slight variation per match (脗卤10Hz) so repeats don't sound identical
- Attack: 10ms (fast start)
- Decay: 70-140ms

**Combo Milestone Sounds:**
- 3-combo: Single ascending tone (200芒聠聮300Hz)
- 5-combo: Chord progression (200, 300, 400Hz in sequence)
- 10-combo: Triumphant 0.5s stab
- 20-combo: Orchestral crescendo (sustained 1.5s)

**Error Sound:**
- Descending tone (300芒聠聮150Hz) over 200ms
- Slightly dissonant (adds "wrongness" without being jarring)
- Complements the error haptic pattern

**Level Complete:**
- 0.3s silence for impact
- Then orchestral sting (0.8s) building to peak
- Tempo matches celebration animation (faster sting = faster visuals)

**UI Sounds:**
- Button press: 50ms click (snappy)
- Menu open: 200ms slide tone
- Achievement unlock: Celebratory 0.5s jingle
